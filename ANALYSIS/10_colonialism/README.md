### Additional Series: The Monroe Doctrine — U.S. Empire in the Americas

**Series ID**: `EL-MONROE-SERIES-2026`  
**Status**: Complete — Publish Ready  
**Description**: A 9-part series tracing the evolution of the Monroe Doctrine from its 1823 origins to the present-day competition with China. Examines the mechanisms of U.S. control — from gunboat diplomacy to covert action to economic coercion — and the resistance they have provoked. Argues that the doctrine is not dead but has been transformed from a unilateral assertion to a contested framework in a multipolar world.
