### Overview
This 8-part series examines the Belt and Road Initiative (BRI) as the modern evolution of the 19th-century colonial extraction model. It traces the historical through-line from the Opium Wars and European "Scramble for Africa" to contemporary Chinese infrastructure statecraft, debt diplomacy, and the "Digital Silk Road."
